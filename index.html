Welcome

The HollyWOOD Chapter

Greetings and welcome to the official website of Alpha Phi Alpha Fraternity, Inc., Pi Kappa Chapter — proudly seated at California State University, Northridge. Founded in 1984, Pi Kappa Chapter has spent four decades building leaders, serving our community, and upholding the legacy of the greatest fraternity in the world.

We are proud to call the greater Hollywood and San Fernando Valley community home, and we remain committed to the charge set forth by our seven founders.
