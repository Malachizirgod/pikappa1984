Service & Programs

Alpha Phi Alpha has always believed that scholarship without service is incomplete. Pi Kappa answers that call every week.

National Initiatives

National Programs

Education
Go-to-High-School, Go-to-College

One of the oldest national programs in Black fraternal history. Pi Kappa hosts college prep workshops, school visits, and mentorship sessions to inspire the next generation of Black scholars in the San Fernando Valley.

Civic Action
A Voteless People Is a Hopeless People

We register voters, host civic education forums, and advocate for full political participation. Every election season, Pi Kappa mobilizes brothers and neighbors to exercise their right to vote.

Brotherhood
Brother’s Keeper

Advocating and improving the quality of life for Alpha Phi Alpha’s senior brothers, their spouses and widows, brothers with disabilities, and vulnerable community members.

Mentorship
Project Alpha™

Our brothers serve as role models and mentors for young men through tutoring, career panels, and one-on-one guidance — investing in the leaders of tomorrow right here in Hollywood.
