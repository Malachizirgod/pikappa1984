Become an Alpha.

Membership in Alpha Phi Alpha Fraternity, Inc. is by invitation only, extended to men who demonstrate the highest standards of scholarship and character.

Membership Information

Alpha Phi Alpha Fraternity, Inc. does not solicit memberships. All interest in membership is coordinated through the national organization at apa1906.net. Pi Kappa Chapter at CSUN welcomes interest from men who are committed to scholarship, service, and brotherhood.

General Requirements
  • Enrolled at California State University, Northridge
  • Minimum GPA as required by the national organization
  • Demonstrated commitment to service and leadership
  • Strong moral character and integrity
  • Formal interest submitted through apa1906.net
